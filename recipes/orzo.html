Parmesan Garlic Orzo

Description

A quick and simple recipe that hits the spot. You can't go wrong with this creamy, cheesy pasta dish

Excellent on its own or as a side dish. Easily scalable to suit your number of guests!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 cup uncooked orzo
  • 400mL chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • fresh basil leaves to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Stir in orzo and saute until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in chicken broth and bring to boil. Cover, reduce heat and simmer until orzo is tender and liquid is absorbed (about 15-20 minutes).
  4. Mix in parmesan cheese and chopped basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Plate and garnish with basil leaves.
